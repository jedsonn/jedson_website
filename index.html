Hi! Welcome to my website. I'm an Assistant Professor of Accounting at the University of Texas at Dallas, where I've been since 2020. This summer I'm visiting Bradesco Asset Management as their AI Research Fellow, helping develop applied AI tools.

My research examines how information is produced, regulated, and consumed in financial markets — and increasingly, how new technologies like large language models are reshaping those processes.

I teach Fundamentals of Accounting Analytics; previously, Financial Accounting and Analysis and Introductory Management Accounting. I'm a CPA (Brazil, inactive). I co-founded the AI Accounting Lab at UTD, and outside UTD I organize the BRA (Brazilian Accounting) Workshops.

Before UTD, I held research and teaching positions at UNC Chapel Hill and FUCAPE Business School (Brazil). I review for leading accounting and finance journals. Besides research and teaching, I enjoy spending time with family and being outdoors.
