How this is built

Every record comes from an open metadata API. Crossref supplies DOI records for SSRN, NBER, and other depositing publishers. arXiv supplies preprints through its public API. OpenAlex and Semantic Scholar fill in abstracts where a publisher did not deposit one. Nothing here is scraped, and no publisher site is queried automatically.

What the bullets are

Abstracts are not reproduced. Each entry carries three written summary lines in fixed slots: the setting, what the model actually did, and the headline result. Each entry states who wrote those lines. Author supplied means the authors submitted them. Editor written means the curator wrote them. Generated means a language model drafted them from the abstract and a second pass checked them. Where no abstract was available, the entry carries a link only.

Corrections and submissions

Authors are welcome to send their own three lines, or to ask for an entry to be corrected or removed. Write to jedson.pinto@utdallas.edu.
